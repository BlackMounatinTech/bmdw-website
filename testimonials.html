Our Approach

How We Work With Our Customers

Every job starts with a conversation. Before we get into the work, we want to understand what you're trying to do, your timeline, and what matters most to you about the project. It helps us figure out if we're the right fit, and it gives us a clearer picture of what you're after.

When you call Black Mountain Dirt Works, you're talking to the owner. There's no sales team or coordinator in the middle. It keeps communication simple and lets us make decisions on the spot when we need to.

Communication Throughout the Project

You'll know what's happening on site and what's coming next. Construction sites change — soil conditions, weather, things that turn up once you're in the ground — and when something comes up, we bring it to you before we make a call on it.

Walk the site with us if you want to. If you'd rather just get updates and photos, that works too.

Finishing the Job

Every job gets finished properly. Proper base prep, solid craftsmanship, clean site when we pack up. That's how we run every project.

Built on Reputation

Four years in, a big part of our work comes from repeat customers and referrals — people we've worked with recommending us to their neighbours, their family, their coworkers. We're not the cheapest option on the island and we're not the biggest. We're an owner-operated crew that builds things properly. If that sounds like what you're looking for, give us a call.
