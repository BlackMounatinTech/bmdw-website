Gravel Driveway Extension

Side-yard driveway extension so the homeowner could get their RV around the back of the house. Tight clearance, narrow strip between the house and the fence line — the kind of job where the equipment barely fits. We excavated down to the right depth, laid geotextile fabric to keep weeds out and spread the load, and brought in blue chip gravel in compacted lifts. Clean, tight finish, and a driveway they can actually use to back the RV in without getting stuck.
