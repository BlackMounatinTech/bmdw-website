Ponds, Waterfalls & Water Features

Custom ponds, waterfalls, and water features. Excavation, liner, stonework, and plumbing.

A well-built water feature transforms a yard. A pond, a waterfall, a stream — done right, it looks like it's always been there. Done wrong, it leaks, the pump fails, the rocks shift, and you end up with an expensive eyesore. We build water features that look natural and run properly for years.

What's Included

  • Custom pond design and excavation
  • Waterfalls and natural-looking streams
  • Pond liner installation (EPDM and concrete)
  • Natural stonework and boulder placement
  • Pump systems, plumbing, and filtration
  • Aquatic planting beds and edging
  • Water feature maintenance and repair
  • Integration with existing landscaping

How We Do It

1. Design & Layout

We work with you on the size, shape, and look of the feature. We figure out where the water comes from, where it goes, and how to make it look like a natural part of your yard rather than something dropped in.

2. Excavation & Liner

We excavate to the right depth and shape, install proper underlayment and liner material, and prep the area for stone placement. The liner is what keeps the water in. We don't cut corners on it.

3. Stonework & Plumbing

We place the boulders and stone to look natural, install the pump, plumbing, and filtration system, and fill the feature. Then we test it, tune it, and walk you through how to take care of it.

Why It Matters

Water features are part construction, part art. The technical side — excavation, liner, plumbing, electrical — has to be done right or the feature fails. The artistic side — stone placement, edging, planting — is what makes it look like it belongs there instead of sitting on top of your yard. We do both sides properly.
