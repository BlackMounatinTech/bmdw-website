Concrete Driveway Addition

Driveway addition — new concrete section poured off the existing driveway to expand the usable area. The trick on add-ons is making the new section look like it was always part of the original, not like something bolted on. We dug out the new area, prepped and compacted the base, formed it up to meet the existing driveway, and poured. Clean joint, smooth finish, and tied into grade properly so water runs where it should.
