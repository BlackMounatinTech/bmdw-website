Excavation & Landscaping in Comox

Serving Comox and surrounding areas on Vancouver Island.

Comox combines waterfront living with rural acreage, and both come with unique site challenges. Coastal properties deal with drainage and erosion. Acreage owners need land clearing, road building, and site prep before anything else can happen. We've worked throughout the Comox Valley and know what the ground does here — especially through the wet months.

Black Mountain Dirt Works is an owner-operated excavation and landscaping company serving Comox and all of Vancouver Island. We work directly with homeowners and property owners on everything from backyard transformations to full site preparation for new builds. Every project gets the same standard — the standard we'd want on our own property.
