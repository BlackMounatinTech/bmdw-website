Tree Removal

Safe removal of trees, stumps, and root systems. Clean site when we're done.

When a tree needs to come down, it needs to come down safely. The wrong cut, the wrong fall, the wrong equipment — and you can damage your house, your fence, your neighbour's property, or worse. We take every tree removal seriously and bring the right equipment and the right experience to do the job safely.

What's Included

  • Tree removal of all sizes
  • Stump grinding and full stump removal
  • Root system extraction
  • Hazard tree removal in tight spaces
  • Storm damage cleanup
  • Selective removal for view clearing
  • Lot prep for new construction
  • Cleanup, hauling, and disposal of debris

How We Do It

1. Tree Assessment

We look at every tree before we touch it. Lean, weight distribution, location, surrounding obstacles, and what's directly underneath. We plan the fall and the cuts before we start cutting.

2. Safe Removal

We use the right equipment for the size of the tree and the access available. Some trees can come down in one piece, others need to come down in sections. Either way, we control where it lands.

3. Stump & Cleanup

We grind the stump down or pull it out completely, depending on what you want. Then we haul away everything — wood, brush, debris — and leave the site clean and ready for whatever's next.

Why It Matters

Tree removal is one of the most dangerous jobs in construction. Most accidents happen because someone tried to drop a tree without planning the fall properly, or used the wrong equipment, or didn't account for the weight distribution. We don't take shortcuts on tree work. Safe, controlled removal every time.
