Winding Concrete Pathway

Custom concrete walkway through an established residential garden. We excavated a curved trench to follow the natural contours of the landscape, installed and compacted a granular base, and poured concrete with a smooth-finish surface. Connects the front and back of the property without disturbing the existing planting beds.
