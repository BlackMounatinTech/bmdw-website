Excavation & Landscaping in Campbell River

Serving Campbell River and surrounding areas on Vancouver Island.

Campbell River is the gateway to the north island, and construction activity here has been steady. Whether it's new home builds along the highway corridor, waterfront retaining walls, or clearing wooded lots for development, there's no shortage of work. The terrain up here can be rocky and heavily treed, which means proper excavation and site prep are critical before any project moves forward.

Black Mountain Dirt Works is an owner-operated excavation and landscaping company serving Campbell River and all of Vancouver Island. We work directly with homeowners and property owners on everything from backyard transformations to full site preparation for new builds. We try to approach every job the same way — carefully, and with the same attention we'd put into our own place.
