Parking Pad & Retaining Wall

Engineered parking pad construction on a sloped residential lot for full-size RV storage. Scope included bulk excavation, French drain installation with geotextile fabric, multi-course segmental block retaining wall, granular backfill, and final compaction of the parking surface. Built to support sustained vehicle loads with proper drainage behind the wall to prevent hydrostatic pressure.
