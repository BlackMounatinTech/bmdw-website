Retaining Wall Replacement

Tear-out and replacement of a failing residential retaining wall. The existing wall was bowing and structurally compromised. We demolished and removed the existing structure, excavated to undisturbed subgrade, prepped a compacted base, and constructed a new segmental block retaining wall with proper drainage. Holds a three-to-four foot grade change across the full property line.
