Patios & Hardscaping

Natural stone, pavers, walkways, and custom outdoor living spaces.

A good patio turns a yard into a place you actually want to spend time. The right hardscape can change how a property feels, how it functions, and how much you enjoy being outside. But hardscape work is detail work — and most of the patios that fail in five years failed because someone rushed the base prep. We don't rush anything.

What's Included

  • Natural stone patios with custom layouts
  • Concrete paver patios and walkways
  • Custom outdoor living spaces
  • Fire pit areas and seating walls
  • Garden walls and decorative borders
  • Stepping stone paths and walkways
  • Excavation, base preparation, and edging
  • Drainage planning and integration

How We Do It

1. Layout & Excavation

We work with you on the layout, mark it out, and excavate to the right depth. We strip the area, set the slope for drainage, and prep the ground for the base material.

2. Base & Compaction

We bring in crushed gravel and lay it in compacted lifts. Then a layer of bedding sand or screenings, screeded smooth and to grade. This is where most patios get ruined — too thin, not compacted, no drainage. We don't skip steps.

3. Installation & Finishing

We lay the stone or pavers tight, cut to fit any edges, install proper edging, and finish the joints. The result is a patio that doesn't shift, doesn't sink, and doesn't grow weeds out of every joint.

Why It Matters

Hardscape work looks easy from the outside — until you watch a five-year-old patio sink, shift, and grow weeds because the base wasn't done right. We treat every patio like it's going to last twenty years, because it should. Proper base prep, proper edging, proper drainage. That's what separates a patio that holds up from one that has to be torn out and redone.
