Excavation & Site Preparation

Site clearing, earthmoving, trenching, grading, and getting raw land build-ready.

Excavation is the foundation of every construction project — literally. Whether you're building a new home, prepping a pad for a shop, or clearing land for a new development, the dirt has to move first. And it has to move right. Bad excavation work is invisible until it isn't, and by then it's expensive to fix. We do it properly the first time so the rest of the project has a solid place to start.

What's Included

  • Bulk excavation and earthmoving
  • Site clearing, grubbing, and topsoil stripping
  • Cut and fill operations to engineered grades
  • Trenching for utilities, foundations, and drainage
  • Rough grading and pad preparation
  • Hauling and disposal of excavated material
  • Compaction and final grade work
  • Coordination with utilities and surveyors

How We Do It

1. Site Assessment

We walk the property, look at the existing conditions, identify any obstacles, and figure out the most efficient approach. Every site is different. Some need more drainage planning, others need more careful clearing, and some are straightforward.

2. Excavation & Earthmoving

We bring in the right equipment for the job and get to work. We move dirt where it needs to go, strip topsoil and stockpile it for reuse if needed, and shape the site to the elevations the project calls for.

3. Grading & Finishing

We bring the site to final grade, compact where compaction is needed, and leave it clean and ready for the next phase — whether that's a foundation pour, a building pad, or a finished landscape.

Why It Matters

Excavation done wrong creates problems that haunt a property for years — settling foundations, drainage issues, water in basements, slopes that fail. We've seen all of it. The cost of doing it right the first time is always less than the cost of fixing it later. We bring the experience and the equipment to handle whatever your site throws at us.
