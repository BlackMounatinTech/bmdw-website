Natural Boulder Retaining Wall

Replacement of a failed concrete-bag retaining wall with a structural natural boulder wall. We removed and disposed of the original wall, prepared the subgrade, and constructed a new gravity-based retaining wall using imported blast rock. Each boulder placed by excavator and locked in tight for long-term structural integrity.
