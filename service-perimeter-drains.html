Perimeter Drains

French drains, perimeter drainage systems, and foundation waterproofing.

Water is the number one enemy of any building. If your basement is wet, your foundation is failing, or water is pooling around your house — you need a proper drainage system. We install drains that actually work, the right way, the first time. Not patches, not band-aids — actual drainage that solves the problem permanently.

What's Included

  • French drain installation around foundations
  • Perimeter drain systems for new and existing homes
  • Foundation waterproofing and membrane installation
  • Drainage repair and replacement
  • Sump pump installation and tie-ins
  • Yard and surface drainage solutions
  • Trenching, pipe installation, and gravel beds
  • Drainage troubleshooting on existing systems

How We Do It

1. Diagnose the Problem

We figure out where the water is coming from and where it needs to go. Wet basements, foundation issues, and yard pooling all have different solutions. We don't install a system that doesn't fit the problem.

2. Excavate & Install

We dig the trench to proper depth and slope, install drainage fabric, lay perforated pipe in a gravel bed, and tie everything into the right outflow. Slope is critical — water has to flow downhill or the system doesn't work.

3. Backfill & Test

We backfill properly with the right material, restore the surface, and make sure the system handles water before we leave. No leaks, no pooling, no second visits.

Why It Matters

A bad drainage system is worse than no drainage system. We get called out all the time to fix drains that someone installed without proper slope, without fabric, or without the right outflow — and the homeowner has been dealing with a wet basement ever since. Drainage is one of those things that has to be done correctly or it's a waste of money. We do it right.
